## What Is an NFT?

An NFT (non-fungible token) is a unique digital asset stored on a blockchain representing ownership of a one-of-a-kind item — a song, album, or collectible.

[image: NFT digital music collectible concept]

---

## Six Ways NFTs Benefit Musicians

### 1. Authenticate and Monetize Your Work

Create an NFT of your music — no label, no distributor, no middleman.

### 2. Create New Revenue Streams

Tokenize related merchandise — limited edition vinyl, signed posters, exclusive artwork.

### 3. Connect Directly with Fans

Offer exclusive, one-of-a-kind experiences that build loyalty.

---

### 4. Sell Limited Edition Physical Copies

The token authenticates ownership while the physical product delivers the experience.

### 5. Distribute Digital Copies Directly

No platform taking a cut, no algorithm deciding who hears your music.

### 6. Offer Exclusive Content and Experiences

Behind-the-scenes footage, private concerts, unreleased tracks, production stems.
