I frequently help artists with distributing and monetizing their music, and one of the most critical points I emphasize is the need for a strategy.

## Be Proactive About Licensing

Having a strategy allows you to actively identify and pursue licensing opportunities — sync licensing for film and TV, video games, and interactive media.

---

## License Strategically

License some tracks for a one-time fee while reserving others for recurring royalty structures. With a plan, you maximize earnings.

---

## Distribute With Purpose

Make some music available for free on streaming while charging for downloads or physical copies. Be intentional about reach vs. revenue.

[image: Music distribution strategy flowchart]

---

## Stay on Top of Deadlines

Without a plan, it's easy to miss crucial deadlines or overlook details that result in lost revenue.

---

## Build Industry Relationships

Track who you've pitched to, when, and what the response was. This builds stronger connections with music supervisors and key industry players.
