I frequently work with musicians who are skilled and passionate but lack the business knowledge needed to turn that passion into a sustainable career. The most important lesson: approach your music like you're running a company.

## Organization Is Everything

Keep accurate records of your finances. Set goals and formulate a strategy. Stay current with developments in your industry, technology, and culture.

[image: Music career planning dashboard or spreadsheet example]

---

## Build Your Team

No successful career is built in isolation. Your team doesn't have to be formal — a friend on social media, a producer who helps with mixes, a photographer who shoots content.

---

## Get Your Finances Right

Have an honest understanding of your income and expenses. Create a budget. Develop multiple revenue streams — merch, streaming royalties, live performance fees, sync licensing, brand partnerships.

---

## Marketing Is Non-Negotiable

Build a strong online presence and use social media strategically — not just posting, but engaging, analyzing, and adjusting.

---

## Be Dependable

Be punctual. Be responsive. Meet your commitments. The artists who build lasting careers are the ones people want to work with again.
