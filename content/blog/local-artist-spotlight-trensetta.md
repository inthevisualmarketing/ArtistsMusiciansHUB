## Who is Trensetta?

Thomas Miller AKA Trensetta is a Texas-based Hip-hop Artist with smooth vibes and brings that energy. Originally from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, raised in Kenosha and San Antonio.

**Instagram: @Trensetta**

---

## The Interview

**Q. Where are you from?**

Born in Milwaukee Wisconsin raised in Kenosha and San Antonio.

**Q. When and what inspired you to start recording your music?**

I started writing very young in like the 4th grade. I came home after having a very bad day and just took my moms notebook and started writing away. It started as a stress reliever now it's a career.

---

**Q. What are some of your family member's musical interests and abilities?**

I never met my grandfather on my fathers side but my grandmother always tells me stories about him. He worked with Rick James back in the day — he even wrote the baseline for Superfreak. Crazy right?

**Q. Which famous musicians do you admire? Why?**

Nipsey Hussle — he was a man of the people and the people really loved him.

**Q. What are your fondest musical memories?**

Just freestyling in school at the lunch table. I love to showcase my talent at times.

---

**Q. Do you perform in public?**

I've performed at art galleries, bars, clubs etc. I really love the clubs though — just the vibes and energy alone is crazy, it's such a rush.

**Q. How do you handle mistakes during a performance?**

I just roll through it. People don't know you mess up unless you show them, so just be professional about it and keep it moving.

**Q. Where do you expect to be in the next 2 years?**

Only time can tell. I'm putting all my time and money into my career so at the top I guess.
