## Who is Nakaki?

Nakaki is an R&B and Hip-Hop artist from San Antonio's North Eastside. With a sound built on vibe creation and authentic emotion, she draws from influences ranging from Xxxtentacion to Giveon to UMI.

**Instagram: @Nakakiiii**
**Streaming: Nakaki on all platforms**

---

## The Interview

**Q. Where are you from?**

San Antonio. North Eastside.

**Q. When and what inspired you to start recording your music?**

Hearing how I actually sounded on a mic. I was like damn that's me?

**Q. Which famous musicians do you admire? Why?**

Top 3: Xxxtentacion, 6lack, and UMI. They're masters of vibe creation from the vocals to the music videos — they're perfect.

**Q. Which famous musicians have you learned from?**

Brent Faiyaz. The way he moves on his own independently is admirable.

---

**Q. What are your fondest musical memories?**

My first time performing "Can't Relate."

**Q. Who are your favorite musicians? Groups?**

Sonder, Xxxtentacion, Smino, UMI, Giveon, Drake, and 6lack.

**Q. Do you perform in public?**

Absolutely whenever I can, to be honest.
