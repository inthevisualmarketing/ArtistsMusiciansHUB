## Who is Suavee?

Suavee was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico to Dominican parents, raised in Louisiana and Texas. The name comes from the Spanish word "Suave" — confident, charming, smooth. Currently a full-time soldier in the Army National Guard.

**Listen: li.sten.to/OfficialSuavee**

---

## The Interview

**Q. Where did your name come from?**

From the Spanish word "Suave" — confident, charming, and smooth, which is what Suavee portrays in his songs.

**Q. What famous musicians have you been inspired by?**

Michael Jackson, Drake, Travis Scott, Lil Wayne, Romeo Santos, and Tory Lanez.

**Q. What do you do outside of music?**

Full time soldier in the Army National Guard. When I'm not on the front lines I can be seen enjoying my free time in San Antonio.

---

**Q. What is your dream tour?**

All major cities and countries around the globe, amazing new talent opening up, a floating stage, merch selling out, special guests from every place, choreography, and crazy visual effects.

**Q. What changes in the industry have impacted you?**

The deaths of inspirations like Pop Smoke, Juice WRLD, and Takeoff have really impacted the way I see the music industry.

**Q. What is your favorite era of rap/hip-hop?**

Late 90's and early 2000's is undoubtedly the best era of music — period!

**Q. What are your future plans?**

Expanding the brand, more shows, more supporters, and dropping bangers!
