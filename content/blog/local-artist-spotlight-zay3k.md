## Who is Zay3K?

Zay3K is a San Antonio artist who found music in college after years of excelling in sports without true passion. Once he started creating, it clicked — natural, driven, and building from the ground up alongside his crew.

---

## The Interview

**Q. Where are you from?**

San Antonio.

**Q. When and what inspired you to start recording your music?**

When I was coming up in high school everybody told me to make music but I never actually wanted to until I started college. I did sports and stuff and I was always good at everything but I didn't have passion for it. When I decided to do music it felt natural.

---

**Q. Which famous musicians do you admire? Why?**

Hans Zimmer for the way he's able to depict a specific feeling, experience or emotion using all kinds of sounds.

**Q. What are your fondest musical memories?**

My best memories are the times when I see someone genuinely moved by what I'm doing. I remember it all.

**Q. Who are your favorite musicians? Groups?**

Deftones, Xxxtentacion, Lil Tracy, Shy Glizzy, Juice WRLD and Lil Dude.

---

**Q. How do you balance your music with other obligations?**

I just have a strong why. Anything I do has a purpose, I know where I'm going and I know what'll put me further. Realistically everything I'm doing is going back to music anyways.

**Q. Where do you expect to be in the next 2 years?**

In the next 2 years I expect to be taking over the music scene and cultivating a new way of thinking and living that transfers to my supporters and the youth.
