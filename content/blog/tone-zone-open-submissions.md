The Tone Zone — AMH's free showcase for San Antonio artists — is officially open for new submissions.

## What Is The Tone Zone?

A curated directory of 50+ San Antonio independent music talent. Completely free for artists and fans. No subscription, no fees, no gatekeeping.

[image: Tone Zone artist gallery on the AMH platform]

---

## How to Submit

1. Visit our Contact page
2. Select "Tone Zone Submission" as your inquiry type
3. Include your artist name, genre, and links to your music
4. Must be born in or represent San Antonio

---

## What We Look For

- Original music
- Active social media presence
- Quality recordings
- Genuine connection to San Antonio

---

## Timeline

We review submissions weekly. If selected, your profile appears within 7-10 days.

---

## It's Free — Always

The Tone Zone will always be free. No strings, no catches.

*Submit your music through our Contact page today.*
