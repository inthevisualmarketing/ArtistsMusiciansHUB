## What Are Cymatics?

Cymatics is the study of how sound waves can create visual patterns and shapes. When waves pass through a medium like liquid, sand, or salt, they create visible geometric patterns.

[image: Cymatic patterns formed in sand on a vibrating plate]

## Three Ways Producers Can Use Cymatics

### 1. Patterns as Musical Inspiration

Study how different frequencies create different visual structures — let the physics of sound guide the music.

### 2. Visualizing Sound Waves in the Studio

Software tools let producers see sound waves in real-time, making mixing and sound design more intuitive.

### 3. Creating Visual Experiences

Cymatic visualizations synchronized with music — for videos, live performances, or social media content.

## Why It Matters

Sound isn't just something you hear — it's something you can see, feel, and shape.
