The streaming economy is shifting — and independent artists need to pay attention.

## The Landscape

Streaming platforms have been under increasing pressure to improve payouts for independent artists.

[image: Streaming revenue comparison chart across platforms]

---

## What's Changing

Platforms are experimenting with artist-centric payment models, where your subscribers' fees go directly to the artists they actually listen to — rather than being pooled across all artists.

This is potentially huge for independent artists with dedicated fanbases.

---

## What Artists Should Do

- Diversify your income streams
- Build direct relationships with fans (email lists, merch, live shows)
- Register all works with your PRO (BMI, ASCAP, SESAC)
- Keep your metadata clean — lost royalties from bad metadata is a real problem

---

## The Bigger Picture

Smart artists treat streaming as one piece of a larger revenue puzzle. Sync licensing, live performance, merchandise, and direct fan support all contribute to a sustainable career.
