Some artists perform. Some produce. Drunk Wizdumb does both — and the balance between the two is what makes his work compelling.

---

## Producer First

Before stepping to the mic, Drunk Wizdumb built his reputation behind the boards. His production style blends classic boom-bap sensibilities with modern trap textures, creating beats that feel both nostalgic and current.

Working with artists across San Antonio's scene, he developed an ear for what makes a track connect — tight low end, space for vocals, and hooks that stick. That production knowledge carries directly into his own music.

---

## Artist Second (But Not Lesser)

When Drunk Wizdumb steps into the artist role, you hear someone who understands song structure from the inside out. His verses are crafted with a producer's attention to rhythm and flow, fitting into the beat rather than fighting against it.

---

## The San Antonio Connection

As a core member of the AMH community, Drunk Wizdumb represents the collaborative spirit that defines the 210's music scene. He's not just building his own career — he's helping build the careers of artists around him through production, mentorship, and genuine support.
