## Who is Supa 2Hunnid?

Tre Williams AKA Supa 2Hunnid is a Texas-based artist that will captivate your attention every song and performance. Be sure to check out his single "New Bag" and follow his journey.

**Instagram: @Supa2hunnid**

---

## The Interview

**Q. Where are you from?**

From Fort Riley, Kansas.

**Q. When and what inspired you to start recording your music?**

Grew up around music / into music.

**Q. Which instruments do you play?**

None actually, but I know 1 song on piano.

**Q. What was the first tune(s) you learned?**

Balancing all the music things and my life things.

**Q. Which famous musicians have you learned from?**

Biggie Smalls, "Ready to Die."

**Q. What are your fondest musical memories? In your house?**

Nah, I used to freestyle in the halls and at lunch.

**Q. How do you handle mistakes during a performance?**

I pretty much just freestyle all the time.

**Q. How often and for how long do you practice?**

I try to work on music everyday, whenever I get the chance.

**Q. How do you balance your music with other obligations?**

I usually do work on music at night.

**Q. Where do you expect to be in the next 2 years?**

More consistent than I am right now.
